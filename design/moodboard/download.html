Welcome

The aim of the Cambridge Mathematics Education Project is to help to make A-level mathematics a rich, coherent and stimulating experience for students and teachers.

If you are new to the site, then you might like to start by exploring the tube map or the pervasive ideas, using the links in the bar at the top. You might also find it helpful to read the descriptions of the resource types that we’re using.

The circled stations on the tube map have the most resources, so they are good starting points.

Teachers might like to read our very short introduction to the site.

Feedback wanted

This website is currently a pilot site, so please do understand that it is under development. We are actively seeking feedback from teachers about our resources, especially based on experiences of using them with students. We are working closely with a number of ‘partner schools’, and are also keen to receive feedback from other schools. To send your thoughts, please .
